The Taxila Story

Over a Decade of Nurturing Excellence & Valor

Founded with a vision to build a strong foundation for children aspiring to serve the country and achieve academic feats, Taxila Academy has evolved into Andhra Pradesh's premier residential coaching institute. We recognized that qualifying for schools like Sainik School, Rashtriya Military School (RMS), or Navodaya requires more than standard schooling.

It demands a focused combination of mental aptitude, physical discipline, and robust values. Our student-centered philosophy makes learning engaging and builds the persistence needed to conquer competitive boundaries.

  • Structured Residential Living: Emphasizes self-reliance, timing, and fitness.

Milestones of Growth

Our Journey

2014

Foundation Year

Taxila Academy was inaugurated in Ravulapalem with just 15 students focusing purely on Sainik entrance prep.

2016

First Selection Milestone

12 out of 15 cadets qualified and secured admission into Korukonda Sainik School, establishing our core credibility.

2019

Expansion of Streams

Launched residential preparation batches for Jawahar Navodaya (JNVST) and AP Gurukulam exams to cater to more students.

2021

Launch of Hybrid Programs

Introduced online classrooms and digital testing interfaces to support distance cadets across Andhra Pradesh.

2024

300+ Selections Landmark

Crossed the landmark milestone of helping over 300 students qualify for various Defence and Residential institutes.
