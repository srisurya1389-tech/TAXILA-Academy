We welcome students from Class 2 onwards. Our primary foundation program (Class 2–5) builds the academic base, while coaching streams for Sainik School, Navodaya, RMS, and AP Gurukulam start from Class 3–4 depending on the target exam.

Yes. Taxila Academy is an English-medium institution. All classes from the primary level (Class 2–5) are taught entirely in English, which directly prepares students for the English sections of the Sainik School, RMS, and JNVST entrance exams.

Taxila Academy has both day-scholar and residential batches. The residential programme follows a structured daily schedule with supervised study hours, physical training, and faculty access — mirroring the environment students will face after selection.

Our fees are transparent and competitive. We offer term-wise and annual payment options. Scholarships up to 50% are available for merit and financially deserving students. Contact us for a detailed fee structure specific to your course.

Our residential campus offers clean dormitories, nutritious meals (3 times daily), 24/7 security, supervised study hours, PT grounds, and a dedicated medical room. Parents receive weekly progress reports via WhatsApp.

Yes — live online classes via Zoom/Google Meet for students who can't relocate immediately. Online students get the same study material, recorded lectures, mock tests, and WhatsApp doubt-support as residential students.

Proprietary printed modules developed exclusively for Sainik, Navodaya, RMS and Gurukulam exams — concept books, 10+ years of previous papers, practice sets, and monthly mock booklets — all included in the course fee.

Full-length mock tests every weekend plus chapter tests on weekdays. In the final 3 months, we increase to 3 tests per week. Detailed subject-wise analytics are shared after every test.

Yes — merit-based scholarships up to 50% fee reduction for students who excel in our internal entrance test. Special financial assistance is available for SC/ST/OBC students and wards of armed forces personnel.
